# ISO-27001-SOC-2-ISO-42001-Integrated-Compliance-Templates
Most SaaS teams running ISO 27001, SOC 2, and ISO 42001 audits collect the same evidence three times because their templates are separate.
This integrated template set fixes that. One control set, mapped across all three frameworks — so your team does the work once and satisfies all three auditors.
Built for:

SaaS startups preparing for their first ISO 27001 or SOC 2 audit
Teams adding AI governance (ISO 42001) alongside existing security compliance
Solo compliance leads and small GRC teams without a full-time auditor
Companies selling to enterprise customers who demand certified security

3. Master Evidence Checklist
Collect each piece of evidence once and map it across all three frameworks automatically.
Organisation-Level Evidence (all 3 frameworks):

Scope statements (ISMS + AIMS)
Policies: InfoSec, AI, Risk, Change, Incident, Supplier
Risk register + treatment plans
Asset inventory + data classification
Access control evidence (MFA, reviews)
Change management tickets
Logging / monitoring dashboards + alert tests
Incident logs + RCAs + corrective actions
Internal audit reports
Management review records

AI-Specific Evidence (ISO 42001):

AI inventory (systems / use cases)
System cards / model cards
Dataset documentation (rights, retention, lineage)
Evaluation plans + results (safety / bias / security)
Human oversight SOPs
AI monitoring metrics + review cadence


4. Minimal Integrated Checklist (Small Teams)
6 workflows that give you ~90% coverage across all three frameworks:
☐ One combined risk register (security + AI)         — Annual + ad-hoc
☐ One change management workflow                     — Per change
☐ One incident response workflow (tag AI incidents)  — Per incident
☐ One vendor management workflow                     — Annual review
☐ One quarterly access review                        — Quarterly
☐ One internal audit + one management review/year    — Annual
